Greek Salad

Salad

Ingredients
  • Romaine lettuce*
  • cucumbers, sliced
  • cherry tomatoes or a tomato cut into pieces
  • avocado, diced
  • garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
  • feta cheese
  • pine nuts, lightly toasted
  • Greek olives (optional)
  • basil leaves (optional)
Instructions

Arrange on a plate and drizzle with dressing.

Dressing

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/3 cup light olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
Instructions

Place all ingredients in a jar and shake. *Buy Romaine lettuce whole and slice or break into pieces.
