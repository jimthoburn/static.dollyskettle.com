Gluten Free Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups mashed banana
  • 1/3 cup avocado or light olive* oil, melted
  • 3/4 cup honey
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups brown rice flour
  • 1/2 cup almond meal
  • 1 tablespoon coconut flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Instructions

Butter a bread pan and line the bottom with waxed paper and butter the paper. Whisk banana, oil, honey, egg and vanilla in a mixing bowl. Add remaining ingredients and whisk until mixed. Pour into prepared pan and bake in a 375 degree oven for 55-60 minutes. Cook completely before cutting.
*Trader Joe’s Trader Grotto’s Sicilian (cold pressed) Extra Virgin Olive Oil is a blend of three olives from Sicily, packaged in Italy in a beautiful bottle. It’s light and delicious. Store it in the dark and use up within a month.
