Sweet Pepper Salad

Salad

Ingredients
  • spring mix salad greens
  • red, green, orange, purple and yellow sweet peppers
  • green cabbage
  • red onion
  • broccoli florets
  • tomato
  • avocado
Instructions

Cut up the desired amounts of ingredients on a plate and drizzle with the dressing.

Dressing

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup light olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons A Vogel Herbamare Original
  • 2-4 garlic cloves, minced (It’s good with a lot of garlic)
Instructions

Place all dressing ingredients in a jar and shake.
