Published on May 29, 2016

Zucchini Noodle Salad

Salad Ingredients

  • 2 zucchini, cut into ‘noodles’ with a spiral vegetable cutter
  • 15 multi colored grape tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup basil, roll up leaves and slice thin
  • 2 cups broccoli florets, steamed 5 minutes and cooled
  • 1 cup red cabbage. sliced
  • 1 can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice pasta, chilled
  • 1 avocado, diced or sliced
  • 3 – 4 radishes, sliced

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/3 olive oil
  • juice of one large lemon (about 2 1/2 tablespoons)
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced

Instructions

Stir dressing ingredients in a bowl. Place salad ingredients in a large bowl or arrange on a large platter and drizzle with dressing. Gently toss salad.
