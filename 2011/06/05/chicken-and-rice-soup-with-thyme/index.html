Chicken and Rice Soup With Thyme

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive or rice bran oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 14 oz can stewed tomatoes, cut up
  • 2 15 oz cans Swansons Chicken Broth
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme leaves
  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • 1 small roasted chicken, broken into bite sized pieces

Instructions

Saute onion in the butter and oil for three minutes. Add bell peppers, celery, tomatoes, broth, thyme rice. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer 45 minutes or until rice is done. Add chicken.
