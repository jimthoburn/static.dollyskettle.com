Turkey Vegetable Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil or light olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 1/2 packages McCormick Mild Chili Seasoning Mix
  • 1 pound ground turkey seasoned with 1/2 teaspoon salt, browned and drained
  • 1 34 ounces chicken broth
  • 1 each red, yellow, green pepper, diced
  • 1-2 zucchini, diced
  • 2 large russet potatoes, diced (opt.)
  • 3 small carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 7 ounce can chopped green chilies
  • 1 can each garbanzo, black, and pinto beans
  • 4 tablespoons double strength tomato paste in a tube
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt or to taste

Instructions

Saute onions and garlic in the oil for 3 minutes. Add cumin and chili seasoning and stir for 1 minute. Add broth and stir until blended. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to boil, turn down, loosely cover and simmer for 2 1/2 hours or until vegetables are tender.
