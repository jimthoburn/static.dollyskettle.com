Taco Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 28 ounce can mini diced tomatoes
  • 2 16 ounce cans kidney, black or pinto beans
  • 1 15 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh corn or 1 8 3/4 ounce can corn with juice

Instructions

Brown meat with onions and drain. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer 30 min.

Serve with shredded cheddar, cilantro and sour cream.
