Mushroom and Spinach Salad

Salad

Ingredients

  • fresh baby spinach leaves
  • thinly sliced mushrooms
  • slivered red onions or finely diced shallots
  • walnut pieces
  • blue cheese
  • hard boiled eggs, chilled
  • sliced tomatoes
Instructions

Arrange salad ingredients on a plate and drizzle with dressing.

Dressing

Ingredients
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup grape seed oil or light extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon Regina Fine White Wine Vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons honey
Instructions

Put all dressing ingredients in a jar and shake together until smooth.
