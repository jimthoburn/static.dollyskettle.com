Italian House Salad

Ingredients

  • mixed greens
  • mini tomatoes, cut in half
  • green onions, sliced
  • cucumbers, halved and sliced
  • avocado, diced
  • mushrooms, sliced
  • radishes for garnish*
  • Basic Italian Vinaigrette (below)

Instructions

Assemble the salad ingredients on a plate and drizzle with the vinaigrette.

Basic Italian Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Bertolli Extra Virgin Olive Oil (light)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

Put all the ingredients in a jar and shake until blended. Prepare the amount of salad ingredients to your taste and toss with dressing. *To make radish roses, wash and trim ends. Make thin slices down the sides, and a second set “petals” at the base. Do a criss cross at the top. Place in a bowl of ice water. If you make them ahead of time and put them in a bowl of water the refrigerator, the “flowers” will open wider.
