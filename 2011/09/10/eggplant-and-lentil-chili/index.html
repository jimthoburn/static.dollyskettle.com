Spicy Lentil Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 5 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 4 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 lb chicken tenders
  • 2 32oz boxes chicken broth
  • 2 28 oz cans diced tomatoes
  • 4 carrots, diced small
  • 2 zucchini, diced small
  • 1 large eggplant, diced small (you can use the equivalent of any type of eggplant)
  • 1 jalapeno chili, seeded and diced small
  • 1 1/2 cups brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup brown rice, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

Rinse chicken tenders and pat dry, Remove tendons and cut in to small pieces and set aside. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes. Add spices and stir 1 minute. Add chicken pieces and cook until pink is gone. Add all remaining ingredients. Bring to boil, turn heat to low, cover with loose lid and cook 1 and a half hours or until vegetables, lentils and rice are tender.
