Fresh Peas and Summer Vegetable Sauté

Ingredients

  • 1 light green zucchini or other light colored squash, sliced
  • 2 yellow crookneck squash, sliced
  • 1 yellow and 1 orange sweet pepper, sliced
  • 3/4 cup fresh shelled peas
  • 1/2 sweet white onion, sliced
  • 2 ears of corn, kernels removed
  • 1 tablespoon coconut, avocado oil or light olive oil*
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

Melt butter with the oil in a large skillet. Add all vegetables and sauté until tender. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
*Trader Joe’s Trader Grotto’s Sicilian (cold pressed) Extra Virgin Olive Oil is a blend of three olives from Sicily, packaged in Italy in a beautiful bottle. It’s light and delicious. Store it in the dark and use up within a month.
