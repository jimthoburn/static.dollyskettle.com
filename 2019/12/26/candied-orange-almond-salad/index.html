Candied Orange Almond Salad

Published on December 26, 2019

This was inspired by Trader Joes’ “Leafy Greens with Butternut Squash Salad Kit” that has a yummy flavor added to the almonds that wasn’t listed on the package. I was guessing it was orange. The kit has arugula, spinach, Lolla Rosa, radicchio, light-green frisée, candied almonds, dried cranberries, feta cheese and cooked butternut squash.

Orange Almond Salad

Ingredients

  • baby spinach
  • arugula
  • butter lettuce
  • sliced oranges (opt.)
  • orange almonds (recipe below)
  • dried cranberries
  • orange dressing (recipe below)

Instructions

Arrange sliced oranges and greens on plates and sprinkle with almonds and cranberries and drizzle with dressing.

Candied Orange Almonds

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 cups sliced almonds
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange rind

Instructions

Heat skillet and add maple syrup, salt and grated orange rind. When this all bubbles add the nuts. Stir constantly so the orange rind is distributed evenly. The nuts will start off sticky and then after 5 minutes the syrup will crystalize the the nuts will separate. Transfer to plates to cool.

Orange Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup avocado or olive oil
  • 1/3 cup white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 – 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange rind

Instructions

Place all ingredients in a jar or salad dressing bottle and shake until combined.
